Honey Mustard-Glazed Salmon

Nutrition
  1. Calories: 940
  2. Carbohydrates: 83g
  3. Net Carbs: 79g
  4. Fat: 41g
  5. Protein: 45g
  6. Sodium: 1420mg
Buy
Ingredients
12 oz. Salmon Fillets 5.47 oz. Long Grain White Rice
1 Yellow Onion 1 oz. Roasted Sliced Almonds
0.88 oz. Balsamic Vinegar ‚Öó oz. Butter
¬Ω fl. oz. Honey 1 Tbsp. Grained Dijon Mustard
2 tsp. Mirepoix Broth Concentrate 6 Chive Sprigs
Instructions
  1. Prepare the Ingredients. Mince chives.
  2. Halve and peel onion. Cut halves into 1/4" dice.
  3. Pat salmon dry and season flesh side with 1/4 tsp. salt and a pinch of pepper.
  4. Cook the Rice Place a medium pot over medium heat. Add butter, onions, and 1/4 tsp. salt to hot pot.
  5. Stir occasionally until starting to soften, 2-4 minutes.Add rice and stir occasionally until lightly toasted, 1-2 minutes.
  6. Add mirepoix base and 11/4 cups water. Bring to a boil.
  7. Once boiling, reduce to a simmer. Cover and cook until rice is tender, 15-18 minutes.
  8. Remove from burner and fluff with a fork. Stir in almonds and half the chives (reserve 1 tsp. for garnish).While rice cooks, continue recipe.
