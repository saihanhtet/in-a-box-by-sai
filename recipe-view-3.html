Butter Cracker-Crusted Chicken

Nutrition
  1. Calories: 580
  2. Carbohydrates: 33g
  3. Net Carbs: 27g
  4. Fat: 31g
  5. Protein: 38g
  6. Sodium: 1920mg

16 oz. Carrot12 oz. Boneless Skinless Chicken Breasts
1¬Ω fl. oz. White Cooking Wine 1 oz. Butter

4 Butter Crackers 2 tsp. Chicken Demi-Glace Concentrate
0.42 oz. Mayonnaise
Instructions
  1. Prepare the Ingredients Peel, trim, and cut carrot into 1/4" rounds. Coarsely crush crackers. Pat chicken dry and season both sides with 1/4 tsp. salt and a pinch of pepper.
  2. Cook the Carrots Place a large non-stick pan over medium-high heat and add 1 tsp. olive oil. Add carrots, 1/4 tsp. salt, and a pinch of pepper to hot pan. Stir occasionally until beginning to soften, 3-5 minutes.
  3. Stir in 1/4 cup water, honey, wine, and half the butter (reserve remaining for sauce). Bring to a simmer. Once simmering, reduce heat to medium, cover, and stir occasionally until liquid is evaporated, 8-10 minutes.
  4. Remove from burner. Set aside to cool slightly, 5 minutes. Stir in pecans and 1/4 tsp. salt. While carrots cook, continue recipe.
  5. Cook the Chicken Place a medium non-stick pan over medium-high heat and add 1 tsp. olive oil. Add chicken to hot pan and sear until browned, 3-4 minutes per side.
  6. Remove from burner. Transfer chicken to prepared baking sheet. Reserve pan; no need to wipe clean. Spread mayonnaise evenly on chicken, then top with crushed crackers, pressing gently to adhere.
  7. Roast in hot oven until chicken reaches a minimum internal temperature of 165 degrees, 7-9 minutes. While chicken roasts, continue recipe.
  8. Make the Sauce Return pan used to sear chicken to medium heat. Add 2 Tbsp. water and demi-glace to hot pan. Bring to a simmer. Once simmering, remove from burner. Swirl in remaining butter.
